Welcome to Bernardo’s Pizzeria, where every bite is a slice of tradition and a taste of passion. Nestled in the heart of Fishers, our family-owned pizzeria has been serving up authentic, handcrafted pizzas since 2007.

Our Story

Bernardo’s Pizzeria began with a simple yet profound vision: to bring the true flavors of Italy to our local community. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Naples, we embarked on a journey to recreate the authentic pizza experience right here in Fishers. What started as a dream has grown into a beloved neighborhood spot where friends and family come together to enjoy great food and great company.

Our Philosophy

At Bernardo's Pizzeria, we believe that pizza is more than just a meal—it's an experience. That's why we use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. From our hand-tossed dough, made daily, to our rich tomato sauce and premium cheeses, every element of our pizza is crafted with care and dedication.

Join Us

Come visit us at 123 Main Street and discover why Bernardo’s Pizzeria is the go-to spot for pizza lovers in Fishers. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we look forward to sharing our passion for pizza with you.
